    Sitekick Remastered Beta

    You can download Sitekick Remastered's open beta from Google Play, Xbox, or the Microsoft Store.

    WARNING: Sitekick Remastered is still in Beta. All chips, xp, and collection lists will be wiped upon the full release.

    Play on Android (Recommended) Download from the Microsoft Store Get it on Xbox (Experimental)

    Play on Android (Recommended)

    Sitekick Remastered is best experienced on Android. Installed from Google Play, we support new as well as legacy device that run Android 5.1 or higher.

    You can now play Sitekick on your phone, tablet, TV, and even your refrigerator. You're welcome.

    Download from the Microsoft Store

    Don't have an Android? Windows 10 and 11 devices can download Sitekick Remastered from the Microsoft store.

    Get it on Xbox (Experimental)

    Don't have an Android OR a Windows device? You can still play Sitekick Remastered on Xbox.
